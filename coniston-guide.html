Rob & Julie's Guide to Coniston

(a work in progress)

Map

The following map shows major amenities and paths ( - - - ) in the surrounding area. Thurston Bank is indicated by the 🏠 symbol on the left.

All the pubs, cafes, restaurants & takeaways are great, but we've put a ⭐ next to our favourites below.

Pubs

You might pass the 1🍺The Sun Hotel as you walk into town, it's one of many traditional pubs, though the Sun is currently not serving food.

In the centre of town you'll find the 2🍺Black Bull and 3🍺The Yewdale, these are very busy and may be full at peak times.
A little further down the road there's the 4🍺The Crown Inn⭐ this is one of our favourite pubs and you can reserve a table here in advance online, by phone or in person.

Alternatively 5🍺The Ship Inn is on the road heading towards Torver, this is a good bet if you don't have a reservation.
It also provides a good excuse to explore the disused railway path near the cottage as this leads to the rear entrance.

Access the railway path via the gap in the wall as indicated by the blue arrow near the cottage on the map.

If you fancy a longer walk take a stroll down to 6🍺The Coniston Inn⭐ near the lake. This is a little more upmarket and popular and so may be busy.

Cafes

On the main street you'll find the 6☕Green Housekeeper Cafe (not dog friendly) and the 7☕Herdwicks Cafe⭐

On the other street you'll find 8☕Meadowdore Cafe and 9☕Lakes Hot Spot Cafe.

Finally if you fancy a walk you'll find 10☕Berties Kitchen & Bar on the way to the lake, and the 11☕Bluebird Cafe⭐ by the lake itself.

Restaurants:

12🍲Sara's Indian is in the centre of town, note it takes CASH only and is unlicensed but you can bring your own drinks.

13🍲Steam Bistro⭐is very popular, and a booking several days in advance is usually required. It's also unlicensed but again you can take your own drinks.

Takeaways

🐟 Our Plaice is the local fish and chip shop, though it's not open all days, note it only accepts CASH.

The Lakes Hot Spot has a Pizza Hatch that operates in the evenings and Sara's Indian is also a take-away.

Food shops

In the town you'll find 3 shops, the Coniston SPAR is inside the BP petrol station, this also provides Post Office services.

The Coniston Coop is on the main road near the pubs, and Hutchinson's butchers & grocery on the road to Hawkshead.
